# Initial Concept
An offline-first, privacy-focused note-taking and productivity web app that runs entirely on the client with no backend, no authentication, and persistent local storage.# Product Definition: NoApp

## Core Vision
An offline-first, privacy-focused productivity suite designed for individuals who prioritize data sovereignty and minimalist design. The app functions entirely in the browser without any backend, authentication, or cloud dependency, ensuring that all notes and tasks remain local to the user's device.

## Target Audience
- **Privacy Advocates:** Users who demand zero data leakage and total control over their personal information.
- **Academic & Professional Organizers:** Individuals requiring hierarchical folder structures for complex information management.
- **Productivity Enthusiasts:** Those seeking a calm, distraction-free environment for deep work and task tracking.

## Key Features
- **Hierarchical Note System:** Supports infinite nested folders for granular organization.
- **Privacy-Centric Security:** Individual folder locking with unique passwords, utilizing client-side hashing and encryption.
- **Integrated Todo System:** Task management with due dates, browser notifications, and productivity cues.
- **Focus Tools:** Built-in Pomodoro timer and dedicated Focus Mode to eliminate distractions.
- **Data Portability:** Individual note export as Markdown and full database backup/restore via JSON for cross-device migration.

## Design Philosophy
- **"Papery and Calm":** A soft, modern Material Design aesthetic that prioritizes readability and ease of use.
- **Intuitive UX:** A seamless interface that feels fast and responsive, minimizing the distance between thought and digital capture.
